Background


Membership



Elesis
A human warrior from Kanavan and the leader of the Ruby Knights, Elesis joined the Chase in hopes of locating her father Elscud, a Kanavan Tracker who had gone missing after being dispatched in the search for Kaze'aze.
From the beginning

Lire
An elf of Eryuell Island and a member of the Elven Corps, Lire joined the Chase on behalf of the elves of Eryuell to combat Kaze'aze's evil.
From the beginning

Arme
A cheerful Magician of the Violet Mage guild in Serdin, Arme joined the Chase for one simple reason alone: to encounter the evil queen and her magic in order to study it.
From the beginning

Lass
A member of the Silver Cross Thieves and a half-demon, Lass was a child possessed by Kaze'aze. He was first introduced at Partusay's Sea as an illusion and later has his first true encounter with the Chase at Kamiki's Castle. He was finally freed from Kaze'aze's possession at Kaze'aze's Castle upon her defeat. Overwhelmed with guilt at the atrocities he had committed whilst under Kaze'aze's control, he joined the Chase to atone.
Kaze'aze's Castle

Ryan
An elven Druid hailing from the Elven Forest, Ryan was first encountered by the Chase in the form of a Wolf as he fought to defend his homeland. After defeating the Troll who was responsible for the destruction of the forest, Ryan joined the Chase.
Elven Forest

Ronan
A Spell Knight of Kanavan and a member of the Kanavan Royal Guard, Ronan was first introduced at the Gorge of Oath and requested their aid at Gaikoz's Castle, leaving after empowering the group with the spell, Holy Bless. Upon defeating Gaikoz at his own castle, Ronan thanked the group and joined them on their journey to overthrow Kaze'aze.
Gaikoz's Castle

Amy
An Oracle and celebrated Dancer of Xenia, Amy first appeared in Lake Aurora where she spied on the Chase before leaving. She was later encountered at the Temple of Cuatal and after saving Samsara with the help of the Chase, joined them to stop the Ascendant God's mission of chaos.
Temple of Cuatal

Jin
A survivor of the nearly annihilated guild, Silver Knights, Jin was first encountered at the Ruins of the Silver Knights, where he killed an Undead Fighter who was attacking Elesis. After defeating the Black Fairy Queen, Jin joined the Chase in hopes of avenging his fallen brethren.
Ruins of the Silver Knights

Sieghart
A well-known hero of Bermesiah from 600 years ago, Sieghart was nearly killed before being reborn as a Highlander. He was looking to join the Grand Chase and encountered them many times through their travels, only realizing who they are at Xenia Border but only joining them at the Wyrm's Maw.
The Wyrm's Maw

Mari
An amnesiac girl who is encountered at the Temple of Destruction, Mari is in actuality a descendant of an ancient kingdom known as Kounat. She first woke up from her hibernation in an escape pod at the Kastulle Ruins Upper Floor and lacking her memories, wandered in a search. Upon learning about Sieghart's immortality, her interest was perked and she joined the Chase to further her research.
Temple of Destruction

Dio
An Asmodian from Elyos and the chief of the Moderate family, Burning Canyon, Dio was an embassy sent to aid Kounat against the invasion of the Hardliners. Trapped in Aernas after the war, Dio continued to seal the Dimensional Portals left in Aernas. He was introduced and invited to join the Chase in the Relics of Kounat, to which he agreed for the sake of pursuing his own interests.
Relics of Kounat

Zero
An artificial demon created by Oz to wield the Grandark, Zero constantly wandered the world in search of a source of power. He clashed swords with the Chase at the Wyrm's Maw and later with Duel at Thunder Hammer, leaving both times. He has teamed up with the Chase during the Wizard's Labyrinth but has not been encountered since. In the sequel, Zero appeared again and saved the Grand Chase from the mass produced combat drone, joining them afterward.
Sky City

Ley
An Asmodian from Elyos and the young lady of the Moderate family, Crimson River, Ley was sent by her father to search for Dio in order to reaffirm their family's alliance. The Chase encountered her and her butler Jeeves in King Guang's Fen. Realizing that they do not have Dio's whereabouts, she left and was not seen again until the Wizard's Labyrinth. She has joined the Chase by the events of Berkas' Lair.
Prior to Berkas' Lair

Rufus
A Haros from the Underworld, Rufus is a Bounty Hunter and also Lass' half-brother. Whilst completing a job in Aernas, he detected the use of the blue flame and set out to investigate, narrowly missing the Chase at the Nightmare Circus. He has, however, allied himself with the group during the Wizard's Labyrinth and has joined the Chase by the events of Berkas' Lair.
Prior to Berkas' Lair

Rin
A girl from the uncharted village of Gaon and the current incarnation of Agnesia, the goddess of life and purity, Rin was encountered by the Chase in the Relics of Kounat. After realizing that she was being pursued by a powerful demon, Rin was invited to join the Chase for her protection.
Relics of Kounat

Asin
Once a nameless boy who had saved Asin Tairin and nursed him back to health, Asin was taught martial arts by the man he saved. He adopted his mentor's name and weapon after the man died from his injuries during a second monster attack. Seeking to prove his strength, he searched for his mentor's former student Jin until he eventually stumbled upon the Grand Chase and joined them.
Prior to Talin's Revenge

Lime
A Holy Knight of the Great Roose Nation, Lime was accompanying her comrades on their campaign to seal off all Dimensional Portals when she was separated from the group. Lost and wandering around the desert, she was found by the Grand Chase and after learning that they were also sealing the portals, joined them.
Prior to Talin's Revenge

Edel
The Captain of the Knights of Serdin, Edel was researching for information on Dimensional Portals in her search for Adel when she came to know about the Grand Chase. Upon hearing that they were hunting and sealing portals leading to Elyos, she set out to join them and study the portals for clues regarding her missing brother.
Prior to Tower of Disappearance

Veigas
A Magi ordered by the Creator to destroy Aernas, Veigas of the Hardliner family, Terre, fought the prophesied Twelve Disciples and were no match for his overwhelming strength. Upon realizing that the order was false, he left to look for the source of the conspiracy. In the sequel, he was saved by Sieghart, Dio, and Lime in the Purgatory after being held by Pino then he eventually joined their siege.
Purgatory
